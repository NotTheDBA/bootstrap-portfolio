About Me

I am a full-stack .NET and SQL developer with over 20 years of career experience, and I'm continually learning learning new technologies.

My primary development experience has been with T-SQL, and middle-tier COM+ and web services, but I have also worked with ASP.NET websites, SharePoint, and various non-Microsoft platforms. As a manager and technical lead, I have championed and managed the implementation of projects and departmental policies, including: legacy software migrations; .NET and SQL coding standards; source control standards and practices; security policies for SQL; web services design; and database design. I frequently served as an adviser to senior management regarding software purchases, business policies and procedures, and departmental objectives.
